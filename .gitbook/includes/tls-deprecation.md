---
title: tls-deprecation
---

Starting from March 27th, 2023, Luciq will end support for TLS 1.0 and 1.1 gradually with full deprecation happening on April 5th, 2023. A brief timeline of the deprecation can be found below, along with what to expect.

### Timeline

* February 27th: Publication of this docs post.
* March 27th: TLS 1.0 and 1.1 will be disabled for an hour, daily, for 5 days.
* April 5th: TLS 1.0 and 1.1 will be disabled indefinitely.

During the week of March 27, if you experience any related issues, please reach out to our support team.
